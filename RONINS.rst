Not so many as Seven Samurai
----------------------------

Concept
.......

A game with the feel of early 2D Zeldas. Multiplayer, cooperative.

Combined with the plot / theme of Kurosawa's Seven Samurai (as remade, lifted and adapted in The Magnificent Seven and probably others). There will be an attack on a village. Band together with the other players to collect up the crops and build defenses before the assault of bandits.
